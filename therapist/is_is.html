International Student & Immigrant Services

You are not alone! I'm here for you!

As a Korean-born woman living in the United States for over the past twelve years, I know first hand at least some of the challenges and difficulties that international students or immigrants face daily; if we work together, I will extend both my professional expertise as well as some shared human experience.

Language Barrier

Have you had a difficulty with communicating because of language barrier? Learning a new language is not easy. It takes time and practice. It is frustrating when people do not understand what you are saying because of your accent or difficulty with pronouncing certain English consonants or vowels. Also, there are some words in your mother tongue that do not have appropriate translations in English, and thus it is hard to convey what you really mean. Language barrier often causes misunderstanding. However, it has nothing to do with your actual intelligence or ability to grasp the concept behind what others say to you. Therefore, you do not have to feel inferior to native English speakers. You do not have to feel low self-esteem.
