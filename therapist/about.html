Welcome to Awakening Within Healing!

Hello! I'm glad you found me! I'm Jieun Gu, MA, RP.

I am most passionate about empowering clients from diverse races, ethnicities, genders, sexualities, spiritualities, and socio-economic backgrounds. As a Korea-born immigrant, I am culturally sensitive and respectful of clients’ diverse backgrounds. I am also passionate about removing stigma attached to seeking counseling for one's mental health and well-being.

I am a compassionate, intuitive, and dedicated therapist who genuinely and deeply cares about my clients. For me, thanks to my cultural heritage and spiritual practices, it is very natural to put myself in other’s shoes.

After earning my Masters degree in Transpersonal Counseling Psychology from Naropa University, I have worked at Mental Health Partners in Boulder and then took Doctoral courses in Clinical Psychology at Pacifica Graduate Institute in California.
